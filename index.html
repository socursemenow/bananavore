Our Origin

Born From Passion,
Built for a Purpose

In the Philippines, bananas are among the most abundant crops. While the fruits are widely consumed, the stems, blossoms, and peels remain largely underutilized — despite their rich nutritional value and potential for creative culinary applications.

The Spark

Bananavore was born from a deep curiosity and a bold question asked by Stefhanie Ann A. Gontiñas while studying Agribusiness Management & Entrepreneurship at UP Los Baños: what if the waste from banana farming — pseudostems, blossoms, and peels — could become the next great Filipino food?

The Innovation

Bananavore's first product, the plant-based luncheon loaf, celebrates the diverse flavors of Filipino cuisine. Drawing from beloved local dishes — Adobo, Sisig, and more — it offers a healthier, fiber-rich alternative to processed meats while supporting local farmers and reducing agricultural waste.

The Vision

More than a product, Bananavore is a pioneering Filipino brand dedicated to transforming underutilized banana by-products into sustainable, nutritious, and culturally relevant alternatives — upcycling what others discard into something the world needs.

Vision

To be a pioneering Filipino brand in the plant-based food industry, dedicated to transforming underutilized banana by-products into sustainable, nutritious, and culturally relevant alternatives to traditional processed meats.

Mission

Creating Filipino-inspired, plant-based meals that are healthy, affordable, and environmentally sustainable — making plant-based choices more accessible, promoting responsible eating, and empowering local farming communities through fair sourcing and value creation.
