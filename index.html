Hello! We're Bananavore.

Bananavore is an undergraduate thesis startup developed by a BS Agribusiness Management student from UP Los Baños.

We celebrate the full potential of the banana plant by crafting luncheon meat from pseudostems, blossoms, and peels — parts that are often discarded but naturally nutritious and full of flavor.

By choosing Bananavore, you support Filipino farmers, embrace nutritious eating, and enjoy a proudly local plant-based meal.

Filipino-grown. Filipino-inspired.

Our Purpose
Driven by Mission
Vision

To be a pioneering Filipino brand in the plant-based food industry, dedicated to transforming underutilized banana by-products into sustainable, nutritious, and culturally relevant alternatives.

Mission

Creating Filipino-inspired, plant-based meals that are healthy, affordable, and environmentally sustainable — empowering local farming communities through fair sourcing.
