# 🍌 Bananavore

**Fuel Your Day, The Plant-Based Way.**

Bananavore is a Filipino plant-based food brand that transforms underutilized banana by-products — pseudostems, blossoms, and peels — into sustainable, nutritious, and delicious alternatives to traditional processed meats.

---

## About

Founded by **Stefhanie Ann A. Gontiñas**, a BS Agribusiness Management & Entrepreneurship graduate from the University of the Philippines Los Baños (UPLB), Bananavore was born from a simple but powerful question: *What if the future of Filipino food isn't a new discovery, but a rediscovery of what's been in our backyards all along?*

In the Philippines, bananas are among the most abundant crops — yet the stems, blossoms, and peels are routinely discarded. Bananavore upcycles these overlooked parts into a plant-based luncheon meat that's healthy, affordable, and proudly Filipino.

## Mission & Vision

**Vision:** To be a pioneering Filipino brand in the plant-based food industry, dedicated to transforming underutilized banana by-products into sustainable, nutritious, and culturally relevant alternatives to traditional processed meats.

**Mission:** Creating Filipino-inspired, plant-based meals that are healthy, affordable, and environmentally sustainable — making plant-based choices more accessible, promoting responsible eating, and empowering local farming communities through fair sourcing and value creation.
